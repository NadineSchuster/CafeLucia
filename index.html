Our Coffee

The milk for our coffee comes from a local farm. As well as the veggies for our sandwiches and fruits for our yummy juices, too! We support little farms to get the best quality products and reduce long transportation.

We make sure, our coffee comes from fairtrade farms only, so we can guarantee fair and best quality products.

We are powered by green energy. We try to keep our carbon footprint as low as possible!
